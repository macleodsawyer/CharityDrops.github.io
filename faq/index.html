FAQ

Q. What charities do you support?

We donate to six different types of charities, and every month we split your donation among five new ones.

Q. What is your selection process?

The nonprofits we review and accept must be:

  • Pursuing innovative solutions to problems in their fields
  • Working within the communities they serve to implement long-lasting solutions
  • Addressing root causes versus individual symptoms
  • Having a meaningful impact on society, as well as a measurable one
  • Majority of their funds must be allocated to their causes

Q. Who are you?

We are a small team that wants to make the the world a better place. You can learn more about IdeaDrops by clicking here.

Q. Are donations tax-deductable?

Not yet. We are still new and are working to find ways to make them tax deductable for our users.

Q. Do you like turtles/tortoises?

Yes! We love them! Our founder has had a pet tortoise since they were nine and wants to one day open a tortoise habitat.

Q. What about dogs? cats? llamas?

We love them all! That's why we try every month to find an organization that helps all sorts of animals! Without them, there can't be an us!

Q. How can I become a volunteer?

Thanks for wanting to help! You can email the volunteer team at volunteer@charitydrops.org

Q. I'm a nonprofit, can you help me?

We always want to help nonprofits who find us, you can submit a request to nonprofits@charitydrops.org
