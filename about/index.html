About

We are a small team that wants to help you discover and support amazing nonprofits. Every month we find five great organizations, and with your help we help them get the donations they need to continue doing amazing things.

We want to maximize charitable donations, so your donation gets split equally among all five of them. The more you donate, the more they can help make the world a better place in more than one way.

Instead of focusing on just one area such as education, we will be selecting five charities from whole different areas of life. Anywhere from the arts, to medical needs, to poverty, and to human rights. Your donations will be sent to valid and official 501(3)c organizations who are deserving of your donations.

If we had 25,000 people donating $25 a month, we would be donating over $6,630,000 a year to organizations all of the world! That's equal to us signing a $100,000 check to organizations such as Alex's Lemonade Stand Foundation to fight childhood cancer or a $100,000 check to the Fund for Animals to help save thousands of animals from cruelty.

Donate now and we will donate $1 more to a great cause.

For every donation we will raise one more dollar to help make the world an even better place*.
